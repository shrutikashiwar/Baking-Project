Vanilla Drip Cake

  • Vanilla flavoured cake dripped with dark chocolate ganache.
  • Filled choco chips and decorated with chopped peanuts.

Recipe

(SAMPLE RECIPE) Dry Ingredients:

  • All Purpose Flour - 4 cups
  • Baking Powder - 2 tsp
  • Baking Soda - 1 tsp
  • Milk Powder - 24 tsp
  • Powdered Sugar - 2 cups
  • Eno - 3-4 pinches
  • Dark Chocolate - 100gm
  • Chocolate chips - as needed

Wet Ingredients:

  • Unsalted Butter - 2 cups
  • Milk - as needed
  • Vanilla Essence - 1/2 tsp
  • Oil - 2 tsp
  • Whipping cream - 2 cups
  • Food Coloring - as needed

Process for the Base:

  • Add the all purpose flour, baking powder and baking soda in a strainer and sieve them together. Keep this aside
  • Now in a bowl add room temperature butter and beat it with a hand whisk or an electric beater in one direction only.(Note: If you're using an electric one, keep at a particular speed only)
  • After the butter seems light and increased, add the sugar and beat again till dissolved.
  • Meanwhile pre-heat your oven/microwave.
  • Now, make a honey consistency paste of the milk powder by adding milk in it in a separate bowl. Add this paste in the butter-sugar mixture and beat for a few second only.
  • Add the dry ingredients mixture gradually to the wet ingredients and use a hand whisk or a spatula to mix them together. Keep adding small amounts of milk. Do not over beat the batter!
  • After mixing in all the dry ingredients, add the essence and the oil and mix again.
  • Check if the batter is in a pourable consistency close to honey. If not, add some milk.
  • Grease your cake mold and add parchment paper.
  • Add eno on the batter and add few drops of milk on it. Quickly mix it in cut-and-fold manner.
  • Add the batter to the mold and into the oven. Do not let the batter to rest.
  • Set your oven at 300 deg. for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, insert a skewer and check if it's cooked fully. If not, bake again for 5-10 minutes.
  • Take it out of the oven and set it to cool.

Process for the Icing:

  • In a bowl add chilled whipping cream and start beating.(Note: If you're using an electric beater, start from the lowest speed and gradually increase.)
  • Beat till it forms stiff peaks!
  • Now cut the base into 3 layers.
  • Place a cake board and put one layer over it.
  • Wet the layer with milk or sugar-water mixture.
  • Add some whipped cream on the layer and even it out with a palatte knife. Add Chocolate chips over it.
  • Add the second layer and repeat the process.
  • Now add the third layer,wet it and put a large scoop of whipped cream over it. This is our ourter layer.
  • Even this out gently and smoothly.
  • Add whipped cream to the sides and even that out too.
  • Remove the excess cream.
  • In a separate bowl add chopped dark chocolate and 2-3 tsp whipping cream.
  • Melt this and you'll be ready with the dark chocolate ganache.
  • Let it cool for a minute or so and then pour it over the cake.
  • Drippings will be formed on the side. Even out on the top with a clean palatte knife
  • Let this set in a cool place.
  • After setting, add chocolate decorations according to your choice.
  • YOUR CAKE IS READY!
